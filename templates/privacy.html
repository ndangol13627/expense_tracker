Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits, used solely for maintaining the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Spendly Service.
  • Display your expense history and spending summaries to you.
  • Send essential account communications (e.g., password resets).
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may use limited third-party services (such as hosting providers) strictly to operate the Service. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on the Service. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
